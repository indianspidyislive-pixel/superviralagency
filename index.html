05 — FAQ

The questions creators
always ask.

What's your split?

It depends on your current revenue and what services you take. Standard full management is 40–50%. We give you the exact number on the strategy call — no surprises.

Do I need to be making money already?

Not necessarily. We take a small number of high-potential new creators each month. Most of our roster was making between $2k and $50k/mo when they joined.

Will I have to do more content?

Usually less than you're doing now. We optimize what already works and only ask for new content where the data shows it pays off.

How long are contracts?

Month to month after a 60-day onboarding. If we're not delivering, you can walk. We bet on the work.

Are my chats handled by humans?

Yes. Trained, vetted, NDA'd chatters in your time zone. We use AI for speed, but every message that goes out is reviewed by a human on your team.

Do you work with male / gay-niche creators?

Yes. We have dedicated managers and chatting teams for the gay/male niche. It's one of our fastest-growing segments.
